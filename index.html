Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered

Is the pool private?

Yes, Casita Nueve offers an exclusive private pool area reserved solely for your group's enjoyment during your stay.

What is the maximum guest capacity?

Our property can comfortably accommodate up to 20-22 guests for overnight stays.

What amenities are included in the rate?

All rates include access to the private pool, unlimited videoke, complete kitchen facilities, outdoor grill, high-speed WiFi, air-conditioned bedrooms, and free parking.

Can we bring our own food and drinks?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own food and drinks. Our complete kitchen facilities and outdoor grill are available for your use.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Standard check-in is at 12:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Flexible arrangements may be available upon request. Please contact us via Facebook to discuss your specific needs.

How do I book and make a reservation?

Simply click the "Book via Facebook" button and message us directly. Our responsive host will assist you with availability, pricing, and any special requests you may have.
